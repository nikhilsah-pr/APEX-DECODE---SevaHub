<h2>🚀 Overview</h2>
<p>
SEVA-HUB is a hyper-local city services and business discovery platform that helps users 
find and book nearby services quickly and efficiently. It transforms a slow and scattered 
process into a fast, seamless, and user-friendly experience.
</p>

<p><b>💡 “Find and book trusted local services in seconds.”</b></p>

---

<h2>🎯 Problem</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Scattered information across platforms</li>
  <li>No reliable trust or verification system</li>
  <li>No real-time service availability</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Result:</b> Users waste time, vendors lose opportunities.</p>

---

<h2>💡 Solution</h2>
<ul>
  <li>📍 Location-based service discovery</li>
  <li>✅ Verified and trusted vendors</li>
  <li>⚡ Booking in under 30 seconds</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Flow:</b> 🔍 Search → 📋 Select → 📞 Book</p>

---

<h2>✨ Key Features</h2>
<ul>
  <li>🔎 Smart search with categories</li>
  <li>📍 Location-based results</li>
  <li>⭐ Ratings & reviews</li>
  <li>⚡ Quick booking system</li>
  <li>📊 Booking status & history</li>
  <li>🧠 Trust Score-based verification</li>
</ul>
